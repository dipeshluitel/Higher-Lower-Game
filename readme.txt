How to Play:

1. Start the Game: Run the script in your terminal.
2. Comparison: The game will display two individuals (A and B) with their descriptions and countries.
3. Make a Guess: Type 'A' or 'B' to guess who has more followers.
4. Score Update: If your guess is correct, your score increases, and you continue to the next round. If your guess is incorrect,the game ends, and your final score is displayed.
5. Replay: After the game ends, you can choose to play again by typing 'y'.
